Making a Cortado

Ingredients

  • Espresso
    Click here to learn how to make espresso
  • Steamed, Hot Milk

Equipment

  • Milk Frother

Making the Cortado

  1. Steam the Milk:
    Pour four ounces of milk into your steaming pitcher (you’ll only pour about two ounces into your cortado glass).
    For a cortado, you won’t need to create foam so much as just steaming the milk thoroughly.
  2. Pour and Enjoy:
    Carefully pour the steamed milk into the espresso, aiming for an equal ratio of espresso to milk.
    The result should be a balanced blend of strong coffee and creamy milk. Then, drink up!
    Cortados are meant to be enjoyed immediately to preserve the balance of flavors.
